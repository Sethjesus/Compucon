Compucon Visits YUAN (Tsung Tai Tech) & Agrtek to Advance Jetson Edge Video AI Collaboration

Date: September 15, 2025 ｜ Location: Taipei

Key Highlights

Compucon engaged with YUAN (Tsung Tai Tech) and Agrtek on NVIDIA® Jetson™ / IGX Orin™ based edge video AI platforms—covering medical imaging, smart city multi-camera analytics, robotics navigation and sensor fusion, as well as compact edge deployments and commercialization.

Detailed Product Overview

Near — IGX Orin for High-End AI

  • Up to 1705 TOPS with optional RTX 6000 Ada dGPU for hybrid Jetson+dGPU workloads.
  • Dual 100Gbps QSFP, GPUDirect RDMA for zero-copy pipelines and high-bandwidth video IO.
  • Targets operating rooms, endoscopy, ultrasound, and real-time medical AI.
  • Supports NVIDIA Holoscan / Metropolis stacks and enterprise security controls.

Edge — Rugged Industrial AI

  • 34–275 TOPS across Orin Nano/NX/AGX configurations, wide-temp/wide-voltage options.
  • CANBus / EtherCAT / UART / SPI / I²C, optional 10GbE and 4G/5G cellular backhaul.
  • Ideal for smart traffic, city surveillance, logistics yards, and factory automation.
  • 24/7 reliability with remote fleet management and OTA update readiness.

Air — Compact & Modular

  • Palm-sized dual-board design up to 157 TOPS with efficient thermal path.
  • Swappable daughterboards: GMSL, SDI, HDMI, TVI, SDVoE, TRX, GigE Vision.
  • Great for broadcasting kits, UAV payloads, mobile robots and kiosks.
  • Industrial fanless builds with IP-rated enclosures available.

Eye — Intelligent AI Camera

  • Orin NX/Nano onboard; 4K60 encode, HDR ISP, Sony Starvis II sensors.
  • Sub-33ms end-to-end latency; on-device analytics; edge-to-cloud streaming.
  • Ideal for inline inspection, traffic analytics, security, and retail behavior analysis.
  • Built-in Wi-Fi/4G/5G; supports SRT/RTSP/NDI workflows.

Pixel — 10" Medical-Grade Device

  • IEC 60601-1 certified with hospital-friendly I/O and isolation design.
  • 10-inch touchscreen for bedside visualization, triage, and clinical guidance.
  • Multi-view inputs; integrates DICOM/HL7 with real-time AI assist.
  • Designed for OR/ICU/wards — low-noise, compact, and disinfectant-resistant.

Arc — Robotics/Humanoid Platform

  • 9-axis IMU for motion control; supports SLAM, VIO, and sensor fusion pipelines.
  • Interfaces for LiDAR, depth, ultrasonic, force/torque sensors; ROS2 ready.
  • Low-latency control I/O, deterministic loops for manipulation and locomotion.
  • For humanoids, AMRs, AGVs, and UAV autonomy stacks.
